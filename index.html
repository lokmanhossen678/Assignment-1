The Vision of Future

Bangladesh 2.0 aims to redefine the nation’s trajectory by embracing cutting-edge technology, promoting inclusive growth, and ensuring sustainability. With initiatives in renewable energy, digital transformation, and robust infrastructure, the country is set to become a global model of development.

Digital Transformation

Creating a nationwide digital ecosystem to enhance connectivity and access to services for all citizens.

Green Energy

Investments in solar and wind energy to ensure a sustainable and eco-friendly future.

Modern Infrastructure

Building state-of-the-art infrastructure to support economic growth and global competitiveness.

Dr. Muhammad Yunus is a globally renowned economist, social entrepreneur, and Nobel Peace Prize laureate known for revolutionizing the world of microfinance. His groundbreaking work in poverty alleviation through microcredit has empowered millions of underprivileged people, particularly women, to achieve financial independence.Born on June 28, 1940, in Chittagong, British India (now Bangladesh), Muhammad Yunus grew up in a middle-class family. He excelled in academics and later pursued a degree in economics at Dhaka University. His thirst for knowledge led him to the United States, where he obtained a Ph.D. in economics from Vanderbilt University. He later became a professor at Chittagong University in Bangladesh.
